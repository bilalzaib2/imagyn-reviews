# IMAGYN LABS

> The place for experimental ideas that make Imagyn Reviews unlike every
> other review app.

**Rule:** These ideas never block launch. Ship first. Build Labs
afterward.

## Vision

Imagyn Reviews should evolve from a review app into an AI-powered
customer intelligence platform.

## Ideas

### 1. AI Product Consultant ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

AI analyzes reviews and recommends: - Product improvements - Copy
changes - Image improvements - Estimated business impact

### 2. AI Review Assistant ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Ask questions like: - Why are customers returning this product? - What
do customers complain about? - Summarize sizing feedback.

### 3. Review Personas

Group reviews by customer type: - Students - Parents - Professionals -
Athletes

### 4. Review Timeline

Visualize sentiment changes over time.

### 5. Review Heatmap

AI maps review mentions to product parts.

### 6. AI Trust Score

Quality score based on: - Verified buyers - Images - Review quality -
Spam detection - Sentiment consistency

### 7. AI Duplicate Issue Detection

Detect recurring product problems automatically.

### 8. Review Journey

Track opinions from unboxing to months later.

### 9. AI Review Rewrite

Help customers write richer, more useful reviews.

### 10. Industry Benchmark

Compare merchants with competitors and industry averages.

## Product Philosophy

Don't compete by adding more widgets.

Compete by helping merchants make better products and sell more.

## Golden Rule

Launch first.

Listen to merchants.

Release Labs features as premium updates.
